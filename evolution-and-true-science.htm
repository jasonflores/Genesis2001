Evolution and True Science

Darwin & Dawkins demonstrate that a good memory, a penchant for writing, and a preconceived conclusion is no substitute for genuine intelligence. Observations coupled with unprovable theories do not evolve into actual facts. Nor do a string of valid yet discrete observations, well shaken, constitute proof of a complex theory.

Sadly, it is a blind and ignorant faith that is the substance of proof for the evolutionist. It is only sad because they do not recognize this faith and think it is fact. The science of evolution, zoology and biology are largely empirical as opposed to the pure science of physics and mathematics.

Where is the testable hypothesis of evolution? Living things are reproducing in multiple ways every day, but the origin of life apparently occurred only once and long ago. It certainly isn't happening in various parts of the world today. In this sense the faith required to believe in atheistic creation is even greater than the faith required to believe in God's creation. And in this sense to say that "evolution is fact", which is what scientists today are saying, is to be preaching the "gospel of evolution".

It is apparent that we need to coin a new term, for example "faith science". And this science would be as opposed to true science. To depart from the long held and proven tradition of the testable hypothesis and to asset "true facts" based on non testable hypothesis crosses from the realm of true science into a "faith science" of some sort.
